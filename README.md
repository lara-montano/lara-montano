### Oscar Daniel Lara-Montano

Assistant Professor and Researcher at the **Universidad Autonoma de Queretaro**, Mexico. My work focuses on mathematical optimization, metaheuristic algorithms, and their application to chemical process engineering.

**Research areas:** process optimization (NLP/MINLP), metaheuristic development (SBOA), reliability-based design, artificial neural networks for engineering, supply chain design, and techno-economic analysis.

**Selected publications**

- *Success-Based Optimization Algorithm (SBOA): Development and Enhancement of a Metaheuristic Optimizer* — Computers & Chemical Engineering, 2025
- *Design and Optimization of Shell-and-Tube Heat Exchangers through ANN and H-ANN Models* — Chemical Engineering Research and Design, 2025
- *A Meta-Heuristic Approach for Reliability-Based Design Optimization of Shell-and-Tube Heat Exchangers* — Applied Thermal Engineering, 2024
- *Comparison of the Performance of Different Metaheuristic Methods for the Optimization of Shell-and-Tube Heat Exchangers* — Computers and Chemical Engineering, 2021
